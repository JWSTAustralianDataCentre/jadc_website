Mission

The James Webb Australian Data Centre is a formed to support the Australian community to leverage the best science from the limited JWST life time.

Tools

We develop the tools you need. Please contact us and give your ideas!

Meet the Team

Meet the beautiful team behind the JADC. Karl Glazebrook is the PI of the JADC which is funded through his ARC Laureate Fellowship. Colin Jacobs is the chief data scientist at the JADC and is in change of developing tools for data driven exploration with JWST data. Themiya Nanayakkara is the chief astronomer at the JADC and is in charge of providing user support to the Australian community to leverage the best use of JWST.
